About

My Story

Hello my name is Wendy. I am an aspiring UX/UI designer from California. My background lies in Hospitality Management. While I was in the hospitality industry, I was introduced to marketing, communications and graphic design. I came to a realization that my talents were better suited in an environment where my creativity could flourish. My path led me to User Interface and User Experience Design. I am recently finishing up am recently finishing up a one year bootcamp course with CareerFoundry, UI Design with a specialization in Front-End Development. Coming from a hospitality background has enabled me to develop transferable skills that cross over to the UX/UI industry. The ability to listen, emphasize and solve problems to best please a client's or user's needs is pivotable in this industry. My background in graphic design has also allowed me to focus on delivering what a client wants and has honed my organizational, time-management, meeting deadlines and delivering a quality product that I am proud of. I am passionate about design and creating UI designs that are aesthetically pleasing, pixel perfect, and most importantly functional. In the future I hope to continue to perfect my UX/UI Design skills and to further develop my knowledge in Front-End Development. I am ready to bring my skillset to the table to an organization that places value in creating wonderful user experiences and to help solve problems with creative design solutions.

In my spare time you can catch me reading a book, listening to my favorite jams, frequenting an art museum or two, meditating or sharing a great bottle of wine alongside great company.
